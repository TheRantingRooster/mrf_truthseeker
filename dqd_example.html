The Revolutionary Framework

The Big Problem

Traditional systems theory treats a coral reef and a factory farm the same way. But one evolved over millions of years to be perfectly sustainable, while the other was designed to extract maximum profit. The DQD finally gives us tools to tell them apart!

DQD = (DT + ED) / (1 + GA)

The Three Dimensions

🔍 Designer Traceability (DT)

The Question: Can you find the architects?

Natural systems emerge from physical laws. Designed systems have creators with agendas. The US Constitution scores 0.18 (emergent democracy), while GDPR scores 0.81 (designed regulation).

🌱 Goal Alignment (GA)

The Question: Does it work with nature or against it?

Measures how well a system mimics nature's closed-loop, regenerative processes. Forests recycle everything; Bitcoin mining burns energy for digital tokens.

⚖️ Enforcement Dependency (ED)

The Question: What happens when the police go home?

Natural systems self-regulate through feedback loops. Artificial systems often collapse without constant enforcement. Remove patent law and see what happens to Big Pharma!
